OceanFocus Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

OceanFocus does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

All focus sessions, progress, streaks, missions, achievements, settings, and app preferences are stored locally on your device.

OceanFocus does not require an account, does not use third-party tracking, does not show ads, and does not send your personal data to a server.

OceanFocus offers optional in-app purchases through Apple’s App Store. Payments and subscriptions are processed by Apple.
